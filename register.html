Registration Information

Registration includes admission to program sessions, the conference package (conference documentation, badge, program booklet, etc.), coffee breaks, two receptions with hors d'ouevres and a drink, and the conference banquet.

A discounted registration fee is available to members of the International Society for Research on Emotion. Those who are not yet members may apply for membership.

Online registration is available until July 5 (early bird registration until May 15). On-site registration will be available July 27 (17:30 - 20:30) and July 28 (8:00-10:30) and will require cash payment in US dollars.

To register, complete the form below. Please direct questions about online registration to Josh Wondra at jdwondra@umich.edu.
